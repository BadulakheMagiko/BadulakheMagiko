My Plants Vs. Zombies mod!


This is where I would show the crappy trailer I made back in the day, but YouTube won't let me put the video here, so I'll have to put it up like this.


During 2023 I started making this mod, in order to test what could be modified in the game with everything the modding community had done to the game and at the same time give it a shabby style, like the mods of 2012.

The version I ended up publishing is still incomplete and buggy; but due to my loss of interest in the project, plus how frustrating the process of fixing some bugs would be, the project is abandoned until further notice. Here is the page of gamejolt page of the mod.

At first I could have made the main page take you to the mod page on gamejolt, but in the end I decided to make it a section of this page to show something.
